📊 AdventureWorks Sales Analytics Dashboard | Excel Project

Developed an end-to-end Sales Analytics Dashboard using Power BI to transform raw business data into actionable insights and support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Business Objective
The objective of this project was to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends to identify growth opportunities and improve business performance.

🔹 Executive Overview Dashboard
• Tracked Total Sales, Production Cost, Net Profit, Profit Margin, and Total Orders.
• Identified top-performing regions and product categories.
• Monitored yearly sales and profit performance.

🔹 Time Series Analysis Dashboard
• Analyzed yearly, quarterly, and monthly sales trends.
• Identified seasonal sales peaks and revenue fluctuations.
• Calculated YoY Growth % and Highest Sales Year.
• Compared Sales, Profit, and Production Cost trends over time.

🔹 Customer Analysis Dashboard
• Evaluated Total Customers, Repeat Customers, and Customer Retention Rate.
• Identified top customers based on sales contribution.
• Analyzed customer purchasing behavior across different markets.
• Studied customer distribution by region and country.

🔹 Product & Regional Analysis Dashboard
• Identified best-selling and high-profit products.
• Highlighted low-profit products requiring business attention.
• Analyzed product category and sub-category performance.
• Evaluated regional sales contribution and market performance.

📈 Key Insights
• Bikes contributed approximately 97% of total category sales.
• Australia emerged as the highest revenue-generating region.
• 2013 recorded the highest sales and profit performance.
• Customer retention exceeded 86%, indicating strong customer loyalty.
• Identified low-profit products that may require pricing or inventory optimization.

🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI | DAX | Data Modeling | Data Cleaning | Data Visualization | KPI Development | Business Intelligence

✅ Outcome
Successfully designed an interactive 4-page business intelligence dashboard that converts complex sales data into clear, actionable insights for stakeholders, helping drive smarter business decisions.
